# Goals

What Residual is trying to accomplish. Updated as targets become more precise.

## Platforms

- Substack — long-form pieces
- X — shorter form, distribution, conversation

## Current Goals (placeholders — to be made precise over time)

### 1. Right Readers
Reach the stakeholder audiences that matter for this work. Who exactly that is will be defined as we go, but the working definition: practitioners and thinkers in games, economics, and data science who are skeptical by default and hard to move.

### 2. Engagement
Comments, open rates, replies, shares — the metrics that indicate active response rather than passive consumption. Specific targets to be set once we have baseline data.

### 3. Quality
Pieces where the argument couldn't exist without the specific evidence. Writing that shifts how a reader thinks about something. How to measure this is an open question — likely through practitioner response and return readership.

## What We're Not Optimizing For

- Raw pageviews or follower counts as primary signals
- Reach at the expense of precision
- Popularity with the already-converted

## Open Questions

- Who specifically is the ideal reader? (designer, economist, executive, academic?)
- What does "engaged" look like on Substack vs. X — are they different audiences?
- What's a realistic timeline to first meaningful data?
