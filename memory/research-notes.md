## HDBSCAN

- Hierarchical Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise
- Does not require specifying number of clusters in advance
- Handles noise points as outliers rather than forcing them into clusters
- Cluster hierarchy built first; stable clusters extracted from it
- Suited to real-world data with uneven density
