## The Research

- Core method: HDBSCAN clustering of video game players using achievement data and play-style traits
- Algorithm choice was iterative — multiple methods evaluated over ~2 years
- Beakerstreet's view: algorithm type matters less than quality of insight it enables
- Economic implications of player clusters are the focus
