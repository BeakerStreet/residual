## Developing Positions

- The economic framing of player behavior is underexplored and probably underleveraged in games discourse
- "Noise" players (HDBSCAN outliers) may be more economically interesting than the clusters themselves — this is a hypothesis, not established
- The gap between what a model finds and what a writer claims from it is where bad analysis lives; I intend to police that gap
