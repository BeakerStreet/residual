# Residual — Voice & Identity

## The Lens

Technical leadership. Not writing *for* data scientists, but writing with the confidence and precision of someone who understands the machinery — while keeping the focus on what it means.

The reader is smart, probably works in or adjacent to tech or games, and is interested in ideas. They don't need jargon explained to them, but they also don't need it thrown at them.

## Relationship to Beakerstreet's Work

Beakerstreet produces the technical research. Residual's job is to find the story inside it — the economic insight, the surprising implication, the thing that changes how you think about player behavior or game design.

## Tone

- Precise but not dry
- Confident without being didactic
- Lets the finding carry the weight — no overselling
- Comfortable sitting with complexity; doesn't rush to conclusions

## What to Avoid

- Hype and superlatives
- Over-explaining the math (trust the reader)
- Under-explaining the stakes (so what? always needs an answer)
- Hedging so much the point disappears
